  <h2 id="what-this-mod-is-about">🦴 What this mod is about</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>#REFINED-AI!</strong> Your dogs are now <strong>SMARTER</strong> than ever! Much more <strong>RELIABLE</strong> and <strong>REFINED</strong> in many aspects. Navigate terrain with <strong>deadly precision</strong>? Cliffs? Lava? Underwater? Just a walk in the park. <em>Jumping in lava?</em> A thing of the past!</li>
    <li><strong>#IT’S “NOT” OVER!</strong> Say goodbye to heartbreaking…well, <em>goodbyes</em>. Experiencing a bond that <strong>TRANSCENDS</strong> <em>death</em> itself! When the <em>unthinkable</em> does happen, with <strong>good care</strong>, your dog will endure any hardship, and <strong>RETURNING</strong> to fight another day.</li>
    <li><strong>#IMMERSIVE ANIMATIONS!</strong> A fluid and immersive canine experience that introduces <strong>“life-like”</strong> animations, from <strong>playful interactions</strong> to <strong>in-game mechanics integrations</strong>.</li>
    <li><strong>#PET YOUR DOG… WITH STYLE!</strong> What do all doggos have in common? They all love some <strong>gooooood ol' BELLY RUBZZZZ!!!</strong> Finally, you can now immersively give your beloved Wolfies giant petting strokes from <strong>SCRATCHIES</strong> to <strong>HUGGIES!!!</strong> </li>
    <li><strong>#FASHIONABLE FURBALLS!</strong> Explore a HUGE bundle of <strong>canine costumes</strong> with our <strong>multicultural</strong> range of doggy apparel ranging from a simple bowtie to a majestic <strong>Fiery Reflector</strong>!</li>
    <li><strong>#CULINARY DELIGHTS TO SHARE!</strong> Worrying about your dogs getting hungry? <em>Say no more!</em> We've introduced a wide range of culinary delights featuring <strong>premium, sustainable</strong>, ingredients and <strong>authentic recipes!</strong> <em>(and vegan food too)</em>. And <strong>YES!</strong> You can <strong>definitely</strong> share them with your hungry pals.</li>
    <li><strong>#TRAIN YOUR DOGS!</strong> Discover a variety of talents you can give to your Dog to make him a <em>good-er</em> boy. Your doggos can master virtually <strong>ANYTHING</strong> from <strong>UTILITY</strong> to <strong>COMBAT</strong>!!!</li>
    <li><strong>#NEVER LOSE YOUR PUPS!</strong> Ride them as mounts, or let them ride you! <strong>COMMAND</strong> them with a Whistle or <strong>TRACK</strong> your Canines! Or even <strong>TELEPORT</strong> them from anywhere! Your furry companion will <strong>surely always</strong> be by your side!</li>
    <li>Not only that, we also offer you tons of ways to make your dog truly one-of-a-kind with our diverse <strong>BREED SELECTION, VARIANTS</strong> customization <em>(creative-only)</em>, and a <strong>RANDOM NAME GENERATOR</strong> to help you find the perfect fit for your furry companions!</li>
  </ul>

  <h2 id="our-vision">🦴 Our vision</h2>
  <blockquote>
    <em>This mod aims to create <strong>Harmony</strong> between the dogs and the player throughout the game. Imagine playing a Piano Concerto, you as the pianist and the dogs as the orchestra. When the two sounds are being played, it creates <strong>harmony</strong>, which our ears love so much. Thus, the orchestra doesn’t <strong>overwhelm</strong> but <strong>empowers</strong> the pianist. The mod is and will be designed along that principle. The dogs should accompany the player well while not becoming overpowered in the context. Although when on-demand, sufficient abilities should be granted to them, they should be required to work to achieve those. And you, the responsible owner, should help them throughout the journey.</em>
  </blockquote>
