title: Bed Finder
---



> ![Sneak + Right-click to put your dog on your head](images/bed_finder_talent.webp "Sneak + Right-click to put your dog on your head")
> 
> While a Dog with this ability is on your head, your bed will have a yellow outline around it.
> 
> To place the Dog on your head, **<code>Sneak + Right click</code>** on the Dog with a [Bone](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Bone) in your hand. (You can still do this without Bed Finder.)
> 
> The range is **200 blocks per level** but becomes **infinite at level 5**.

## Trivia

- Introduced In the original **Doggy Talents**.
- This talent is useful for marking home location.
- Be careful when carrying your dog through narrow areas! (Allow for 3 blocks high at least.)
