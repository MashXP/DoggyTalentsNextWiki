---
title: Bed Dog
---



> This talent allows you to sleep on your Dog when you forget to bring beds.
> 
> To begin sleeping on your Dog, interact with it using the [Bed Dog Whistle](Whistle) to make it lay down, then interact with it with the whistle again after it has laid down to begin sleeping on it.
> 
> Each successful sleep will cost the Dog some Nourishment Points and cause a cooldown on this talent during which you can't sleep on this dog. Both cooldown time and Nourishment Point cost will be decreased with each level trained and will be removed when maxed out.

## Activation Conditions
In order for the talent to work:
- The owner of the server [allows](Configuration) the use of this talent. (doDaylightCycle = true)
- It must be night time.
- The surrounding area must be unobstructed and vacant.
- [Nourishment Point](Basic Core Stats#Nourishment Points) has to be over 20.
- Sleep Cooldown (Level 1, 2, 3: 3 days, Level 4: 2 days, Max level: no cooldown)
- The dog's size is not too big nor too small. 
## Trivia
