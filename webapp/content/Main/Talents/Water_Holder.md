> ![*Notice the Dog's fur, it loves absorbing water. And the Dog loves shaking it dry.*](images/water_holder_talent.webp "*Notice the Dog's fur, it loves absorbing water. And the Dog loves shaking it dry.*")
> 
> This talent allows your Dog to **store water.**
> 
> If you or your other wolves/dogs are **on fire,** it will run to each and shake its fur, **reducing the burn time or completely extinguishing the target**.
> 
> The higher the level, the more Water the Dog can store, so too the burning effect time that the Dog can reduce!
> 
> 
> 
> You can either use a [Water Bucket](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Water_Bucket) on the Dog to replenish Water or the Dog will automatically absorb Water when it comes in contact with **Rain or a Water source.** You can check the amount of Water left by using **any bucket** on the Dog while holding **<code>Sneak</code>**.
> 
> 
> **Mastering** the skill grants your **Dog's fur Infinite Water Capacity.**

## Trivia

- Introduced in **DTN 1.7.0**.
- This talent is very useful when traveling in the Nether
