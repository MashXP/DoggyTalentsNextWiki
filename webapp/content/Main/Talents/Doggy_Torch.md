---
title: Doggy Torch
---
Allows your dog to automatically place torches from its inventory in low-light areas.

## Detailed Description
Allows your dog to **place down [Torches](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Torch) when the light level is low,** at the cost of one Torch from its inventory ([Pack Puppy](Pack_Puppy)). 

To give it Torches, you can **drop some nearby** and it will pick them up. 

At **level 5,** it **no longer needs any Torches** in its inventory.
