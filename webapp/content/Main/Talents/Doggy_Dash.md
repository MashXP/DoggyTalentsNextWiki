> ![Without Doggy Dash - With Doggy Dash](/images/doggy_dash_talent.gif "Without Doggy Dash - With Doggy Dash")
> 
> Increases your Dog's movement speed.
> 
> 
> 
> Every level grants a 12% increase.
> 
> Mastering it grants an additional 15% increase.

## Trivia

- Introduced In the original **Doggy Talents**.
- This talent is useful for combat (along with [Black Pelt](Black_Pelt) + [Doggy Tools](Doggy_Tools)), and [Wolf Mount](Wolf_Mount).
- Beware when controlling a speedy doggo, you might get yourself in sticky situations!
