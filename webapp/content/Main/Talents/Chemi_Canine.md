---
title: Chemi Canine
---



> When you or your other dogs **receive negative effects,** the dog with this talent will run to the target and try to **absorb the negative effects,** one at a time.
> 
> 
> The effects will accumulate to the dog (without causing harm to it). The **higher the level, the more effects** it can absorb.
> 
> 
> When it accumulates enough, the dog cannot absorb more effects and have to **wait for the accumulated effects to decay**, which will take a while.
> 
> 
> However, when the dog attacks, the target will be **affected by one of the effects absorbed** by it, which also **consumes the effect allowing the dog to absorb again.**

## Trivia

- Introduced in DTN 1.17.2.
