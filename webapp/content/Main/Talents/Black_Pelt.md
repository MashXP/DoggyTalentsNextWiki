---
title: Black Pelt
---
Significantly increases your dog's critical hit damage, making them much more lethal in combat.

## Detailed Description
Increases critical hit rate by half a heart per level, plus an additional heart upon mastery.

![Black Pelt Doggie one-shot a Zombie](black_pelt_talent.gif "Black Pelt Doggie one-shot a Zombie")

## Trivia

- Introduced In the original **Doggy Talents**.
- The talent can be coupled with [Doggy Tools](Doggy_Tools) - Equip it with a sword to further increase its damage output.
