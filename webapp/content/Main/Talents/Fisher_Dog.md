title: Fisher Dog
---
Allows your dog to catch fish while swimming.

## Detailed Description
Whenever your Dog enters the water, it **may catch a [Fish](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Fish)** which it will **give you when it shakes itself dry**.
![Shakey shaky fishy fishy~](fisher_dog_talent.webp "Shakey shaky fishy fishy~")

The **chance** of catching a Fish **increase with level**, and any points in [**Hell Hound**](Hell_Hound) can cause the Fish to be **cooked**.


**Mastering** this skill will allow your Dog to **breathe underwater**.
