---
title: Poison Fang
---



> ![Very effective against Witches!](/images/poison_fang_talent.webp "Very effective against Witches!")
> 
> Your Dog's **attacks will poison its target**, and the **duration of the poison increases with level.**
> 
> 
> At **level 3** your Dog becomes **immune to poison.**
> 
> 
> **Mastering** this skill allows **you to cleanse yourself of all potion effects**, including poison, by **<code>Right click</code>** on your Dog with a [Spider Eye](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Spider_Eye), at the cost of **30 [Nourishment Points](Basic Core Stats)**.

## Trivia

- Introduced In the original **Doggy Talents**.
