---
title: Happy Eater
---



> Increases the [Nourishment Points](Basic Core Stats) your Dog gets from food items by **10%** per level.
> 
> In addition, from **level 2,** your Dog will be less of a picky eater and accept [Cooked Fishes](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Cooked_Fish) on their own.

## Trivia

![No longer be able to eat Rotten Flesh](/images/happy_eater_talent_old.gif "No longer be able to eat Rotten Flesh")

- It was added in the original DT.
- Back then, DTN Dogs can eat Rotten Flesh, but later the Dev's decided that they deserved better quality food.
