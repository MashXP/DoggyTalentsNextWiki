> 
> Allows your Dog to utilize specific tools to help you with certain tasks. Tools can be added to your Dog's toolbar, which can be accessed by **<code>Right-clicking</code>** your Dog with any variation of the Pickaxe.
> 
> The higher the level, the greater the capacity of the toolbar.
> 
> Dogs with a Stone Hoe inside the toolbar along with [Rice Grains](Rice_Grains) or Wheat Seeds will help you harvest **Rice** and Wheat respectively (if both is present in the Dog's toolbar, it will choose left-most item).
> 
> Dogs with any weapon (Axes, Swords, Bows, Tridents and Crossbows) in their toolbar will find and equip the left-most weapon.
> 
> * Arrows for the Bow and Crossbow can be stored either in the toolbar or in [Pack Puppy](Pack_Puppy)<nowiki/>'s inventory.
> * Trident can only be used as a Ranged Attack weapon if enchanted with Loyalty 2 or greater, it can still be used as a Melee weapon otherwise.

### When farming
Make sure you:

- Set your dog's mode to **Docile**.
- Put at least Rice Grains (or Wheat Seed) in [Doggy Tools](Doggy_Tools) Toolbar, not [Pack Puppy](Pack_Puppy)<nowiki/>'s inventory.
- Use Stone Hoe.
- Always Pick First Slot set to False.

## Trivia

- It was added in DTN 1.12.0.
- There's an instance where **Doggy Tools** desync when [Immortal Dogs](Immortal_Dogs) is disabled, it's recommended to keep [Immortal Dogs](Immortal_Dogs) on.
