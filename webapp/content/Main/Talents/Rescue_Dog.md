title: Rescue Dog
---
Allows your dog to prioritize healing you or other pack members when health is critically low.

## Detailed Description
When **you or your other dogs** are at **critical health** and a Dog with this skill is follows you, it will **run to the target** and **attempts to heal the target** with its **hunger**.
![whoops...](rescue_dog_talent.webp "whoops...")


The higher the level, the **higher the heal amount** and the **lesser the heal cost.**
