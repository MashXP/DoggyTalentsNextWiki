---
title: Shock Absorber
---
Provides knockback resistance and reduces damage taken from explosions and sonic booms.

## Detailed Description
This talent increases your dog's **Knockback Resistance.**


The higher the level the less damage taken from **explosions** and **Warden's Sonic boom attack**.

Mastering this grant the Dog complete **immunity to Knockback, explosion damage, and sonic boom**.

## Trivia

- Introduced in **DTN 1.12.0**.
