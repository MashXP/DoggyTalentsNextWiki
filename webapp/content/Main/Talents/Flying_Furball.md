---
title: Flying Furball
---
A Kami-level talent that grants your dog the incredible ability to fly through the skies.

## Detailed Description
*Only Kami-level Dogs can learn this talent !!!*

Allow your Dog to **conquer the limit of the sky!**


The higher the level the faster your Dog flies.

### Flying Control
Since **DTN 1.18.45**, you can manually allow or disallow flying while riding your dog via the dog's configuration. 

## Trivia

- Introduced in **DTN 1.17.2**.
- **DTN 1.18.45** added an option to toggle flying for Dogs being ridden.
- Very OP when combined with [Wolf Mount](Wolf_Mount) talent.
