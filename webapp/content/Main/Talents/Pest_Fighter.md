title: Pest Fighter
---
Automatically inflicts damage on nearby Silverfish to protect your home or mine.

## Detailed Description
Inflicts 1 point of damage to all nearby [Silverfish](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Silverfish) at random ticks.
![PEST BUSTERS!](pest_fighter_talent.gif "PEST BUSTERS!")


Increasing the skill level **extends the range by 3 blocks.**


Mastering the skill **doubles the damage.**
