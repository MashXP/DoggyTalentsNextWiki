title: Shepherd Dog
---
Helps you rustle up and transport nearby livestock over long distances using a shepherd's whistle.

## Detailed Description
**Hold** **the [Shepherd's Whistle](Whistle)** (just hold) and all trained Shepherd Dogs in **[Docile mode](Behaviors/Basic_Core_Stats)** will **rustle up some nearby livestock** (amount based on level), making it **easier to transport livestock over long distances**.
![Ignore de sheep.](sheperd_dog_talent.webp "Ignore de sheep.")


However, this is tiring and costs [Nourishment Points](Behaviors/Basic_Core_Stats), the cost of which is reduced with higher levels of the skill.
