---
title: Dog Behavior Improvements
---

- Enhanced Navigation and Movement: Dogs can now navigate diverse landscapes and swim underwater.
- Improved Hunger Mechanic: Dogs exhibit realistic hunger behaviors and face consequences based on difficulty.
- Saturation Effect: Introduced a saturation system to influence dog health and healing.
- Refined Attacking Mechanics: Dogs attack more accurately and safely, target selection and attack range.
- Mid-Air Damage: Dogs can now deal damage while in the air.
- Improved Fire & Lava Avoidance: Enhanced the dog's fire avoidance behavior.
- Mining Logic Improvement: Avoid going in front of the Owner while the Owner is digging.
- Dog Eating Effect: Added visual and audio effects for dog eating.
- Avoid teleporting in front of the Owner when the Owner is sprinting.
- Armor System: Added a new armor system for dogs, allowing customization and protection.
- Floats if low on Air.
- Smarter Teleport.
- Smarter Modes: You can now have more control of your dogs.
- Improve Find Water if Burning.
- Beg if Hungry.
- Show Health in the dog's name when sneaking.
- Auto-eat from nearby [Pack Puppy](Pack_Puppy) dogs who have eligible food when hungry.
- Stick to [Owner/Flee](Dog_Menu#Info Tab) when on Low Health. (Low Health Strategy.)
- Whistle Mode Picking Interface: Redesigned the interface for a more user-friendly experience.
- Injured Mode: No dogs shall ever die! (Low-Graphics Texture included.)
- Conducting Bone: Added for summoning and recalling dogs.
- Redesigned Dog GUI: Improved user experience.
- Dog Tag Game: Introduced a new game for dogs.
- Team Player Support: Added for dogs with certain talents.
- Revamped Skin System: Introduced more customization options.
- Amnesia Bone: Introduced for detraining, untaming and changing dog ownership.
- Cross-Origin Teleportation: Added a configurable option for cross-dimension dog teleportation.
- Doggy Artifact System: Introduced a new system for equipping artifacts to dogs.
- Animations: Introduced various animations for dogs, including begging, lying down and scratching.
- Name Randomizer: Added for randomizing dog names.
- New Gameplay Mechanics: Added new weapon and quality-of-life improvements.
- Allow ranged attacks, push resistance.
