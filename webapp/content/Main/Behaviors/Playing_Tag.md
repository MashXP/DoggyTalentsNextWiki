title: Playing Tag
---

## Activating the Mode

1. Set your dog's [mode](Behaviors/Basic_Core_Stats) to Docile.
1. Throw a [Snowball](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Snowball) at your dog.
1. Your doggo will start chasing you. 

## Possible Outcomes
If it catches you, there will be a prompt that says: "Dog: Gotcha!". After that it's your turn to catch your doggo.

- If you fail to catch your dog, your dog wins.
- If you manage to outrun your dog, you win.
