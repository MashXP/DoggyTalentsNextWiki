**Upright Piano** is a musical instrument that allows you to provide a more sophisticated atmosphere for your canine companions. Whether for a peaceful evening or a grand performance, this compact piano is a perfect addition to any space.

## Obtaining
Press **<code>E</code>** in Creative mode to access the Creative menu.

## Usage
- **<code>Right click</code>** to interact with the piano.

## Trivia
- Introduced in **DTN 1.15.0**.
- Developers have expressed interest in adding more functionality and making the item craftable in survival mode in the future.
