**WTC I: Fugue in C# Minor (BWV 849)** by Johann Sebastian Bach is a music disc in *Doggy Talents Next*. Part of *The Well-Tempered Clavier*, this piece brings a somber and intellectual atmosphere to your jukebox.

## Obtaining
- There is a **0.15% chance** for a dog wearing a **[Bach's Wig](Bach_Wig)** to drop this disc after killing an entity.

## Usage
- **<code>Right-click</code>** on a Jukebox to play the music.
- **<code>Right-click</code>** again to eject the disc.

## Trivia
- Introduced in **DTN 1.15.0**. Removed in **DTN 1.18.28** due to being too heavy.
- The recording features a performance by **Kimiko Ishizaka**, provided to the **CC0 - Public Domain**.
- Along with the *Art Of Fugue* disc, this forms the initial set of classical music recordings added to the mod.
