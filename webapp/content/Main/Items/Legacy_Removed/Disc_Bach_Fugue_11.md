**Art Of Fugue: Contrapunctus XI (BWV 1080)** by Johann Sebastian Bach is a music disc in *Doggy Talents Next*. It features a rich, complex fugue provided to the public domain.

## Obtaining
- There is a **0.15% chance** for a dog wearing a **[Bach's Wig](Bach_Wig)** to drop this disc after killing an entity.

## Usage
- **<code>Right-click</code>** on a Jukebox to play the music.
- **<code>Right-click</code>** again to eject the disc.

## Trivia
- Introduced in **DTN 1.15.0**. Removed in **DTN 1.18.28** due to being too heavy.
- The recording features a performance by **Kimiko Ishizaka**, which was donated to the **CC0 - Public Domain**.
- This disc is part of the "Bach set" included in the loot tables for dogs wearing historical wigs.
