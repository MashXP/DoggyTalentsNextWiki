**Ryoshima Coast** (arranged by DashieDev for Organ) from the game **[Okami](https://www.capcom-games.com/okami/)** is a majestic music disc in *Doggy Talents Next*. It captures the adventurous spirit of the coast where the Sun Goddess Amaterasu once roamed.

## Obtaining
- There is a **0.15% chance** for a dog wearing a **[Bach's Wig](Bach_Wig)** (or other historical wigs) to drop this disc after killing an entity.

## Usage
- **<code>Right-click</code>** on a Jukebox to play the music.
- **<code>Right-click</code>** again to eject the disc.

## Trivia
- Introduced in **DTN 1.15.0**. Removed in **DTN 1.18.28** due to being too heavy.
- This disc is a direct tribute to the game *Okami*, which has been a primary influence on the development of many features in *Doggy Talents Next*.
- The arrangement was created specifically for the mod by **DashieDev**.
