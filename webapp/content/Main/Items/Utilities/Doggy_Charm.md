**Doggy Charm** is an extraordinary consumable item that instantly grants you the companionship of a fully trained dog. It's the perfect way to jumpstart your adventures with a loyal friend at your side, and it even allows for quick on-the-fly changes to your dog's visual variant.

## Obtaining
### Upon Creating A New World
If you have **<code>enable_starting_items</code>** set to **<code>true</code>** on your server configuration file. You can use the [Starter Bundle](Starter_Bundle) automatically presented in your inventory when you first join that world.

## Usage
- **<code>Right click</code>** the ground to spawn a new trained dog.
- **<code>Sneak + Right click</code>** on an existing dog with the charm to cycle through all available **Classical Variants**.

## Trivia
- Introduced in the original **Doggy Talents**.
- The Doggy Charm has historically served as the icon for the old Doggy Talents Next logo and has been featured prominently in banners and community watermarks.
- The item's modern sprite is inspired by the **[Charms](https://okami.fandom.com/wiki/Tools)** found in the game **Okami** <ref>https://okami.fandom.com/wiki/Tools</ref>.
