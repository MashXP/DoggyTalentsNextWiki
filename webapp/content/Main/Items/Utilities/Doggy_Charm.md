**Doggy Charm** is a consumable item that spawns a trained dog.

## Obtaining
### Upon Creating A New World
If you have **<code>enable_starting_items</code>** set to **<code>true</code>** on your server configuration file. You can use the [Starter Bundle](Starter_Bundle) automatically presented in your inventory when you first join that world.

## Usage
- **<code>Right click</code>** to use the item.
- **<code>Sneak + Right click</code>** on your dog to change its variant.
