**Starter Bundle** is a beginner-friendly utility item that provides a comprehensive collection of tools to kickstart your journey with Doggy Talents Next. It ensures that every new trainer has the essential gear needed to tame, track, and command their first canine companion with ease and confidence.

Upon opening, the bundle grants the following items:
- **[Doggy Charm](Doggy_Charm)** x1
- **[Whistle](Whistle)** x1
- **[Radio Collar](Radio_Collar)** x1
- **[Canine Tracker](Canine_Tracker)** x1
- **[Stick](https://minecraft.fandom.com/wiki/Stick)** x1
- **[Bone](https://minecraft.fandom.com/wiki/Bone)** x1

## Obtaining
### Upon Create New World
If you have **<code>enable_starting_items</code>** set to **<code>true</code>** on your server configuration file, you will receive this bundle upon creating a new world.

### Creative Menu
The bundle can be found in the Doggy Talents Next creative tab.

## Usage
- **<code>Right click</code>** the item in your hand to open the bundle and receive its contents.

## Trivia
- Introduced in **DTN 1.17.0**.
- The item's sprite is modeled after the **[Treasure Chests](https://okami.fandom.com/wiki/Treasure_chest)** from the game **Okami**, a significant inspiration for the mod's development and aesthetics.
