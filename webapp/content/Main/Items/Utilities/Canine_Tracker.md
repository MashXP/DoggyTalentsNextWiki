**Canine Tracker** is a powerful utility item that allows you to pinpoint the exact location of any dog you own. Whether you're searching for a lost pup in the wild or coordinating a large pack across a base, this device ensures your focus remains on your canine companions, keeping them safe and accounted for.

![Canine Tracker Display](/images/canine_tracker_display.png)

## Usage
- **<code>Right click</code>** to open the tracking interface.
- Navigate the list to select the dog you wish to track.
- The device will then guide you toward the dog's current position.

## Trivia
- Introduced in **DTN 1.15.0**.
- The tracking text's color intensity shifts based on distance; the further away the dog is, the less vibrant the text becomes.
- When you are within **6 blocks** of the tracked dog, the tracker will display the message: "**<code>&lt;Your dog&gt; is here</code>**".
- Before **DTN 1.18.28**, the Canine Tracker could only track dogs that were specifically equipped with a **[Radio Collar](Radio_Collar)**.
- The item is a direct reference to the **Canine Tracker** spiritual device given to **Amaterasu** by **Princess Fuse** in the game **Okami** <ref>https://www.ign.com/wikis/okami/Princess_Fuse_and_the_Canine_Warriors</ref>.
