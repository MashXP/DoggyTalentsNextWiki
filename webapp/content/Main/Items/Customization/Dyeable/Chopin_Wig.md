**Chopin Wig** is a dyeable accessory that gives your dog the elegant and romantic appearance of a famous pianist. It's the ideal choice for dogs that prefer a more "fancy" and classical aesthetic while they listen to their favorite music discs.

## Usage
- **<code>Right click</code>** on your dog to quickly equip the item or use the **Style tab** within the [Dog Menu](Dog_Menu#Style).

## Trivia
- Introduced in **DTN 1.11.0**.
- The wig is a reference to **Frédéric Chopin**, a Polish composer and virtuoso pianist of the Romantic era <ref>https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fr%C3%A9d%C3%A9ric_Chopin</ref>.
