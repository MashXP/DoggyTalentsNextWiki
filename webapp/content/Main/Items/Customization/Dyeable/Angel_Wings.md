**Angel Wings** is a dyeable accessory that adds a touch of heaven to any canine, making them look as sweet as they act. Perfect for dogs who have already earned their wings by being the bestest boys and girls. 

Pair it with the [Angel Halo](Angel_Halo) for the full celestial experience!

## Obtaining
Angel Wings is not currently craftable in survival mode.

## Usage
- **<code>Right click</code>** on your dog to quickly equip the item or use the **Style tab** within the [Dog Menu](Dog_Menu#Style).

## Trivia
- Introduced in **DTN 1.18.19**.
