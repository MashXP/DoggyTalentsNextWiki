**MIDI Keyboard** is a dyeable accessory that lets your dog carry their tunes wherever they go. Whether they're a budding composer or just love a good beat, this keyboard is the perfect way to show off their musical talent.

## Obtaining
### Crafting
This item is not currently craftable in survival mode. (yet...)

## Usage
- **<code>Right click</code>** on your dog to quickly equip the item or use the **Style tab** within the [Dog Menu](Dog_Menu#Style).

## Trivia
- Introduced in **DTN 1.18.19**.
- The model was created by **MrBlueYeti** - the Owner of BetterDogs Resource Pack <ref>https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/texture-packs/better-dogs</ref>.
- Pair it with **[MrBlueYeti's Goose](MrBlueYeti's_Goose)** for the full musical experience!
