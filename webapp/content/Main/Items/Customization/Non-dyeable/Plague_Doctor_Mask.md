**Plague Doctor Mask** is a non-dyeable equipable item that makes your dog spooky and professional.

![Spooky and Professional](/images/plague_doctor_mask_display.png "Spooky and Professional")

## Obtaining
### Crafting
<recipe />

Obtaining this item requires Leather to unlock the recipe.

## Usage
- **<code>Right click</code>** on your dog to quickly equip the item or use the **Style tab** within the [Dog Menu](Dog_Menu#Style).
## Trivia
- Introduced in DTN 1.17.2.
