**Head Band** is a versatile accessory that allows your dog to take on various mysterious disguises. Whether they're trying to blend into a crowd or just looking for a new look, these headbands offer several unique patterns.

| No. | Image | Pattern | ID | Description | Craftable? |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | ![](/images/head_band_blank.png) | Blank | `doggytalents:head_band_blank` | | Yes |
| 2 | ![](/images/head_band_mystery.png) | Mysterious | `doggytalents:head_band_mystery` | Who's that wolfy boi? | No |
| 3 | ![](/images/head_band_highhh.png) | Highhh | `doggytalents:head_band_highhh` | The Dev's favorite emoji. | No |

![](/images/head_band_display.png)

## Obtaining
- Currently you can only craft the **Head Band Blank**; the others can be obtained via the Creative menu.
<recipe />

*Requires Paper to unlock the recipe.*

## Usage
- **<code>Right click</code>** on your dog to quickly equip the item or use the **Style tab** within the [Dog Menu](Dog_Menu#Style).

## Trivia
- Introduced in **DTN 1.17.2**.
- This item is a reference to the **Moon Cave** arc in **Okami**, where Amaterasu (Ammy) must obtain and wear an **[Imp Mask](https://okami.fandom.com/wiki/Imp_Mask)** to camouflage herself and infiltrate Orochi's lair.
- In the original game, players could use the Celestial Brush to draw their own designs on the mask, which enabled them to pass by guards undetected. The devs wanted to implement this feature to DTN as well.
