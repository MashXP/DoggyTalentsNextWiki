**Divine Retribution** is a legendary reflector that burns with the intensity of the sun. It is a direct reference to the **Divine Retribution** weapon from the game **Okami** <ref>https://www.capcom-games.com/okami/</ref>, which has been a major inspiration for *Doggy Talents Next*.

## Usage
- **<code>Right click</code>** on your dog to quickly equip the item or use the **Style tab** within the [Dog Menu](Dog_Menu#Style).

## Trivia
- While purely cosmetic for most, it captures the essence of the Sun Goddess Amaterasu (mother of us all).
- Introduced in **DTN 1.17.2**.
- This item might accidentally cook your food.
- More information about the original inspiration can be found on the **Okami Wiki** <ref>https://okami.fandom.com/wiki/Divine_Retribution</ref>.
