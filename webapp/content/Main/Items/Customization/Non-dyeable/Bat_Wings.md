**Bat Wings** are a non-dyeable accessory that gives your dog a "batty" and nocturnal appearance. Perfect for spooky season or for dogs that aspire to be dark guardians of the night.

## Usage
- **<code>Right click</code>** on your dog to quickly equip the item or use the **Style tab** within the [Dog Menu](Dog_Menu#Style).

## Trivia
- Introduced in **DTN 1.17.2** as part of the **Halloween Update**.
- Despite their name, these wings do not grant the dog the ability to fly; they are purely cosmetic unless used with specific [airborne talents](Flying_Furball).
