**Firefighter Hat** is a non-dyeable accessory that ensures your dog is geared up and ready to face the heat of any adventure. Safety first, treats second!

| No. | Hat | Name | ID |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | ![](/images/firefighter_hat_red.png) | Red Fire Fighter Hat | doggytalents:firefighter_hat_red |
| 2 | ![](/images/firefighter_hat_blue.png) | Blue Fire Fighter Hat | doggytalents:firefighter_hat_blue |

## Obtaining
This item is not currently craftable in survival mode. (yet...)

## Usage
- **<code>Right click</code>** on your dog to quickly equip the item or use the **Style tab** within the [Dog Menu](Dog_Menu#Style).

## Trivia
- Introduced in **DTN 1.18.33**.
- The combination of the **[Blue Fire Fighter Suit](Firefighter_Suits)** and the **Blue Fire Fighter Hat** (which is black with blue highlights) is a direct reference to the popular anime franchise **Fire Force** <ref>https://myanimelist.net/anime/38671/Enen_no_Shouboutai</ref>.
