**Throw Stick** is a throwable item that lets you play and interact with your doggo!

## Obtaining
### Crafting
<recipe id="throw_stick" />

## Usage
> Make sure your dog is in [DOCILE mode](Basic_Core_Stats#Modes), **<code>Right click</code>** in the distance to throw it, and watch as the dog fetches it and brings it back to you!
> * It will be covered in dog drool ![](/images/throw_stick_wet.png), so you'll have to **<code>Right click</code>** to wipe it off before you can throw it again. <ref>https://doggytalents.fandom.com/wiki/List_of_Items_and_Equipment</ref>

## Trivia
- It was added in the original Doggy Talents.
- It functions identically to the [Throw Bone](Throw_Bone).
