> Once your dog is level 60, you can still raise your dog's "kami level" with Kami Treats! This maxes out at level 30, at which point your dog is fully trained and acquires a nifty aura (can be enabled via [Configuration](Configuration)).

**Kami Treat** is the ultimate reward for your canine companions, allowing them to transcend their physical limits. Once a dog reaches level 60, these divine treats can raise their **[Kami Level](Leveling_Systems#Kami_Leveling)** up to a maximum of 30, unlocking a majestic aura and proving their status as a true god among dogs.

In Creative Mode (as of **1.18.28+**), the Kami Treat will now **one-shot** Dogs to their maximum Kami level.

## Usage
- **<code>Right click</code>** on your dog to raise its **Kami Level** by 1.
- In Creative Mode (as of **DTN 1.18.28+**), feeding a **Kami Treat** will instantly max out a dog's Kami level.
- Maxing out Kami Level unlocks a majestic aura (configurable via **[Configuration](Configuration)**).

## Trivia
- Introduced in the original **Doggy Talents**.
- It was originally called **Dire Treat** (the name was changed in **DTN 1.18.0**).
- A potential overhaul of the treat system is being considered for future development.
