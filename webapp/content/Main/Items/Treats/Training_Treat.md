> **Training Treat** is the first treat you'll need to make.
> * Each treat raises your dog's level by 1 and also raises your spendable points by 1
> * This treat is no longer usable once your dog is level 20. <ref>https://doggytalents.fandom.com/wiki/List_of_treats</ref>

**Training Treat** is the fundamental gateway to DTN, serving as the very first treat every aspiring dog trainer must craft. It is the essential "key" that unlocks the potential within any wolf, converting them into a loyal and talented companion ready for a lifetime of adventure.

Each treat raises your dog's level by 1 (and 1 spendable point) until they reach **level 20** <ref>https://doggytalents.fandom.com/wiki/List_of_treats</ref>.

<recipe />

## Usage
- **<code>Right click</code>** on your dog to use the item.
- As of **DTN 1.18.0+**, a Training Treat can be used to directly convert an untamed wolf (even an angry one) into a **Doggy Talents Next** Dog. See more in **[Getting Started](Getting_Started)**.
