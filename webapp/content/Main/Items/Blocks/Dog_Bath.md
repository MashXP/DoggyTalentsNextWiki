## Trivia
- Introduced in the original **Doggy Talents**.
- Dog Bath used to have a function:

> When standing in the dog bath, the dog seems to continuously shake, flinging water particles and occasionally dropping fish if you have spent skill points on the Fisher Dog skill, all you have to do is get your dog sitting in the bath. <ref>https://doggytalents.fandom.com/wiki/List_of_Items_and_Equipment</ref>

- Although it currently serves as a decoration block, DashieDev suggested that it might return and have more applications in the near future.
- While currently primarily decorative, dogs will occasionally trot over to the bath for a quick drink of water, adding a touch of life and comfort to any dog-friendly home.
