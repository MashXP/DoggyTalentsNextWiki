**Uncooked Rice** is a material used to craft Rice related items.

## Obtaining
### Grinding

- **<code>Sneak + Right click</code>** [Rice Grains](Rice_Grains) on a [Grindstone](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Grindstone) to dehull the rice.

## Used In

- [Uncooked Rice in a Bowl](Uncooked_Rice_in_a_Bowl)
- [Koji](Koji)
