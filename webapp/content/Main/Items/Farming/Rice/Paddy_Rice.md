**Paddy Rice** is a rice-related crafting material harvested from [Rice Crops](Rice_Grains).

## Obtaining
### Farming

- **<code>Right click</code>** [Rice Grains](Rice_Grains) on Farmland and let it fully grown into Paddy Rice.
