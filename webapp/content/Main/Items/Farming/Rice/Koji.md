- It was added in DTN 1.18.0.
- Can be brewed into [Sake](Bottle_of_Sake) with a [Water Bottle](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Glass_Bottle#Water_Bottle) and some Sugar inside a [Brewing Stand](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Brewing_Stand).
- Koji - 麹 is a filamentous fungus (a mold) used in East Asia to saccharify rice... in the making of alcoholic beverages such as sake and shōchū, and also to ferment soybeans for making soy sauce and miso. <ref>[Aspergillus oryzae - Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aspergillus_oryzae)</ref>
