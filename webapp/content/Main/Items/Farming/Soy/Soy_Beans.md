**Soy Beans** is a soy-related crafting material.

## Obtaining

### Killing Hostile Mobs

- The item has a random chance of dropping from certain hostile mobs (Zombies, Skeletons, Spiders, Creepers,...) when a [trained](Getting_Started) dog deals the final blow.

### Farming

- It'll drop from Soy Crops when harvested.
- **<code>Right click</code>** [Soy Pods](Soy_Pods) to obtain Soy Beans.

## Used in

- [Dried Soy Beans](Dried_Soy_Beans)
- [Miso Paste](Miso_Paste) 
- [Natto](Natto)

## Usage

- **<code>Right click</code>** on [Farmland](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Farmland) to plant Soy Crops.
