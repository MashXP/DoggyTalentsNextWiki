**Soy Pods** is an item harvested from [Soy Crops](Soy Beans).

## Obtaining

### Farming

- You can harvest 1-3 Soy Pods per fully grown Soy Crop.

## Used In

- [Dried Soy Pods](Dried_Soy_Pods)
- [Edamame](Edamame)

## Usage

- **<code>Right click</code>** to use the item.
