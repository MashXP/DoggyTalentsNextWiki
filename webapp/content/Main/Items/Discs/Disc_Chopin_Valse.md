**Valse Du Petit Chien** (also known as the "Minute Waltz") by Frédéric Chopin is a lively music disc in *Doggy Talents Next*. Its fast-paced tempo is said to be inspired by a dog chasing its own tail, making it the perfect theme for a playful pup.

## Obtaining
- There is a **0.15% chance** for a dog wearing a **[Chopin Wig](Chopin_Wig)** to drop this disc after killing an entity.

## Usage
- **<code>Right-click</code>** on a Jukebox to play the music.
- **<code>Right-click</code>** again to eject the disc.
- Dogs will become "hyperactive" and start spinning when this disc is played!

## Trivia
- A placeholder version (Musescore recording) was introduced in **DTN 1.16.0**.
- The current high-quality recording was performed by **DashieDev** on his Kawai upright piano and post-processed by **MashXP**. It was added in **DTN 1.17.3**.
