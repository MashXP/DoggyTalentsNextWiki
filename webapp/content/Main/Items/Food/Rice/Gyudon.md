**Gyudon** is a **very delicious** food item that fills up a full hunger bar and gives you:

- **Regeneration II** for **20 seconds**.
- **Resistance II** for **40 seconds.**
- **Absorption II** for **20 seconds.**
- **Instant Health I.**

Your dog can also eat this, so don't be shy to share it with your furry pal!
## Obtaining
### Crafting

| Ingredients | Recipe |
| --- | --- |
| [Rice Bowl](Rice_Bowl) + [Onsen Tamago](Onsen_Tamago) | ![](/images/gyudon_crafting.png) |

## Usage
- **<code>Right click</code>** to use the item.
## Trivia
- It was added in DTN 1.18.0.

- The description of the item is a reference to a very famous Japanese restaurant that the Devs really like.
