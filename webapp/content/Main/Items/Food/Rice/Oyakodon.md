**Oyakodon** is a **very delicious** food item that fills up **7 hunger points** and gives you:
- **Resistance I** for **40 seconds.**
- **Absorption II** for **20 seconds.**
- **Slow Falling I** for **20 seconds.**
Your dog can also eat this, so don't be shy to share it with your furry pal!
